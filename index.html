How we Proceed – 5 Steps Mantra

Discovery Meeting

This is an initial opportunity to discuss the project in detail and take the brief. During the discovery meeting we learn about the client's personality, their style and their individual lifestyle, tastes and aspirations. We determine Scope of work in project, give them the exposure of advanced technologies & discuss the scope of its implementation in their project. We'd also discuss outline budgets and time scale so that the project can progress smoothly. Space symphony also discuss the variety of budget range with client and decide for the specified project. Facilitate client with the exposure of varied design styles followed in interiors. Pick the one suiting to clients personality & taste.

Concept Design

In response to the client's brief a concept scheme is created for discussion. This will be presented in the form of design boards, draft layouts showing space utilisation, any structural modifications and furniture layouts. We produce mood boards or presentation sheets to demonstrate design style, colour schemes, wall treatments along with options for samples and finishes.

Detail Design

Once all elements in the concept design are agreed with the client, the project moves to the Detail Design and Specification stage. We start making working & technical drawings This procedure begins with crafting of accurate layout plans, all the working & technical drawings for the accurate implementation & execution on site. Every element of the scheme is designed and specified for the client to sign-off. Samples are presented for discussion and client final approval.

Project Supervision

The overall programme is drawn up and agreed with the client. Progress of the project is managed and monitored on site on a regular basis and any issues arising in the course of the project are resolved in a timely and pragmatic manner in order to maintain quality and programme. Any snagging issues are resolved quickly and efficiently to the client's satisfaction.

Dressing & Accessorisation

At the finishing stage, every small detail is looked into with supreme vigilance and dedication to give you a wholesome experience. We closely monitor the output at every stage until the closure. This is where the vision becomes reality. All work by trades has been completed. Now the furniture is delivered, window treatments are installed, rugs laid, accessories placed and art hung.
